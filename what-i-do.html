What I Do

I 3D model and 3D print a variety of items. If I need a part to solve a problem, like a headphone holder designed specifically for my desk, I design and print it.

If someone wants an existing 3D model printed for them, I'll print it. Want something that doesn't exist yet? I take commissions, I can model and print it for you. Beyond printing, I can finish and paint items post print, and have even made functional cosplay props.
