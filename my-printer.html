My Printer

My printer is a Creality Ender 3 Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) 3D Printer. It is a consumer grade printer that has been modified to achieve excellent print quality for its relatively cheap price point.

Some of the modifications I have made include:

  • Custom built enclosure
  • Glass print bed
  • Upgraded hot-end fan
  • Upgraded roller bearings

While the printer itself sits in the sub $200 range for 3D printers, I believe the upgrades I have made to it push its quality beyond the entry level range.

Ultimately, I have decided that cost to benefit of putting any more money into this printer would be unfavorable, so plans to add online print and monitoring functionality have been cancelled. Instead, the funds would be of better use towards a newer printer with higher precision and quality out of the box.
